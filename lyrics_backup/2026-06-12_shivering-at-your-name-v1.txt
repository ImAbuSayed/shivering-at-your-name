[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the embers burning in the dark
Every time your shadow crosses my heart
Just a whisper and my defenses fall
I am leaning into your midnight call

[Verse 1]
We are tracing the lines on this wooden floor
Leaving the cold outside the heavy door
Your touch is a spark that I cannot explain
Falling over me like a gentle rain

[Pre-Chorus]
The room gets quiet, the fire is low
I am following the rhythm of your breathing, slow
My hands are trembling, I am losing control
I am trading my secrets just to hold your soul

[Chorus]
And I am shivering at your name
Caught inside this wild, beautiful flame
You pull me closer, tearing down my walls
I am answering every time your silence calls
